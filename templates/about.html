About CasePulse AI

CasePulse AI is a lightweight extension that connects to MyCase to read case updates, analyze changes using AI, and notify your clients automatically by SMS and email in multiple languages. This is NOT MyCase — it requires your MyCase account to authorize access.

  • Daily automated checks (configurable)
  • AI summarization and multilingual message generation
  • Email via SendGrid (recommended) or SMTP, SMS via Twilio or fallback email-to-SMS
